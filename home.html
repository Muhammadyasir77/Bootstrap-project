Acid reflux occurs when stomach acid flows back up into the esophagus, causing symptoms such as heartburn, regurgitation, and discomfort. It can be triggered by certain foods, lifestyle factors, or medical conditions. Managing acid reflux often involves dietary changes, lifestyle modifications, and sometimes medication to reduce stomach acid production.
